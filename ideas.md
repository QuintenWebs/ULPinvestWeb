# ULP Invest – Design Brainstorm

## Context
A microfinance + entrepreneurship support initiative in the Usambara Mountains, Tanzania.
Part of the Ubuntu Leadership Program. Dark-blue theme requested, inspired by Acumen.org and ubuntuleadershipprogram.nl.

## Selected Design: "Contemporary Impact — Dark Atlas"

This approach best captures the dual identity of ULP Invest: serious financial institution + warm human development organization. The deep navy communicates trust and professionalism, while gold accents evoke the Tanzanian landscape and the warmth of human connection. The asymmetric layout creates visual interest without sacrificing clarity.
